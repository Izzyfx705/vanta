VANTA Story

VANTA was created from the idea that simplicity can still feel powerful. The brand represents quiet confidence — clothing that doesn't need loud graphics or flashy colors to stand out. Every piece is designed to feel clean, modern, and intentional.

The name VANTA is inspired by darkness, depth, and mystery. Just like black absorbs everything, the brand reflects individuality, ambition, and expression without limits. It's for people who move differently, think creatively, and want fashion that feels timeless instead of temporary.

VANTA focuses on:

  • → Minimal luxury
  • → Clean silhouettes
  • → Premium streetwear aesthetics
  • → Confidence through simplicity

The goal is to create pieces that feel effortless but memorable — clothing you can wear anywhere while still feeling distinct.

Refund Policy

Last Updated: May 21, 2026

Welcome to VANTA. We value our customers and aim to ensure satisfaction with every purchase. Please read our refund policy carefully before making a purchase.

1. Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds are only eligible under the following conditions:

  • The item received is damaged, defective, or incorrect.
  • The refund request is made within 7 days of delivery.
  • The item is unused, unworn, unwashed, and in its original condition and packaging.

2. Non-Refundable Items

We do not offer refunds for:

  • Worn or used items
  • Items damaged after delivery
  • Sale or discounted items
  • Custom or personalized products

3. Exchanges

If you receive the wrong size or a defective item, we may offer an exchange depending on stock availability.

4. Refund Process

To request a refund, customers must:

  1. Send an email or DM with:
    • Order number
    • Proof of purchase
    • Clear photos of the issue
  2. Wait for approval from our support team before returning any item.

Once approved and the item is inspected, refunds will be processed within 5–10 business days to the original payment method.

5. Shipping Costs

  • Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the mistake was made by VANTA.
  • Original shipping fees are non-refundable.

6. Order Cancellations

Orders can only be canceled within 12 hours of purchase. After this period, processing may already have begun.

7. Contact Us

For refund requests or questions, contact us through:
